Janissaries to Jet Engines: Sonic Ecologies of the Anthropocene

Bates College — Lewiston, Maine — November 7, 2025 — Prof. Louis Goldford

Um... How did we get here??

  • When did noise stop being “the enemy” of music and become its material?
  • How have we learned to hear chaos, machinery, and nature as music?
  • How has music traced our shifting relationship to the environment — from imitation to participation?
  • Can listening itself be ecological?
